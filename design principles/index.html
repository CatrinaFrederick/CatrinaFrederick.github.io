Principles of Design

Catrina Frederick

NPR

NPR uses repitions in multiple ways to strengthen the design of their site. First all of the typography is set the same in every article preview. Second the thumbnail image is always the same size and placed in the top right corner of the article preview. Third every button link to a video or recording is in the bottom left corner and is the same shade of blue used in the background of the NPR logo

Solly Baby

The Solly Baby mobile site uses the principle of visual hierarchy this is achieved by the use of a semetrical balance having the top image span the page and having an overlay encouraging the user to shop their limited realease item. They also use alignment to draw your eye down the page to the next item for sale. The image for the next item is center aligned with the overlay on the top image.

BBC

The BBC America mobile site exemplifiys excelent use of contrast. The dark background allows for the bright images and light text to really stand out.
