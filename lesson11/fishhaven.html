10th Anniversary of Napoleon Dynamite Celebrated with Errection of Bronze Statue

On June 9th, 2014 the cast of Napoleon Dynamite reunited to unveil a statue commemorating the 10th anniversary of the film that put Preston, Idaho on the map. Written and directed by Jared and Jerusha Hess the film stared many unknowns (Jon Heder as Napoleon) including our small rural Idaho town. The film became an instant cult classic and earned Jared and Jerusha Hess a commendation from the Idaho Legislature in April of 2005 for promoting many of the important hallmarks of Idaho culture. A few of these included the promotion of Idaho's most famous export potatoes by predominately featuring tater tots in the film. The film brought national notariey to Preston High School and incresed tourism. You can read the full HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 29 at the offical wbsite of the Idaho Legislature.
