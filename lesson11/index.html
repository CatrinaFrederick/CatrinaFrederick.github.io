Cold Weather:Snow Could Return to Central Idaho

The National Weather Service has issued winter storm warnings and winter weather advisories for East Idaho because of the storm. The weather service said East Idaho's higher elevations can expect to receive another 1 to 4 inches of snow from the storm on Sunday night before it exits the region. East Idaho's lower elevations could receive a rain-snow mix on Sunday night with snow accumulations of less than an inch.
