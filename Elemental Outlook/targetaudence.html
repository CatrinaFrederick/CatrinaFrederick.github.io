Target Audence

This site is for individuals 18-50 years old who are both residents and non residents looking for information about the local areas for weather and outdoor activities.

The site will be for people from all walks of life and since this site will be a free source of information income and social status will not be a huge factor.

Persona One

Mary is 28 years old and wants to get hre young family outside more often. To do this she wants to start weekly park days with her firends and will plan a small hikes on a weekly to monthly basis for her family of five that consits of her , her husband and their two children ages 2, 4 and 6.

Mary is always on the go taking care of her family and primarally uses her phone to browse the web. She will acess the site from her phone and use it to plan her park days and hikes

Persona Two

John is 40 years old and loves to go fishing. He plans frishing day trips with his buddies and sometimes with his son. He is busy working his 9-5 job and will mostly chek the site from his phone. He will also use the site to check where the best fish are being caugt and the stock times for the lakes, rivers and resiviours.
